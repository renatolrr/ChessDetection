Suicidal Cake Help

Suicidal Cake plays the suicide variant of checkers. In this variant, the player to lose wins - your goal is to lose all your pieces (or not be able to make a move). All other rules are like in English checkers.

Like regular checkers, the game appears to be drawish - but it allows a lot of nice tactics.

Suicidal Cake comes with the 4-piece endgame database. I have computed the entire 8-piece database for the computation of the opening book, because a lot of very lopsided positions are in fact drawn, because neither side can approach the other's pieces due to losing tactics.

You can download a smallish opening book for Suicidal Cake on my checkers download page.
